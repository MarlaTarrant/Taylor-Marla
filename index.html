Ceremony & Reception

Kleine Liefde, Porterville


It is with great pleasure that we invite you to join us to celebrate the beginning of our new life together...


Please read through the following details and kindly let us know if you are able to join us by filling out your
details below, before the 15th of February.


Our wedding ceremony and reception will be held just 15 kilometres outside the warm and picturesque village
of Porterville, nestled away in the foothills of the Groot Winterhoek mountain range. The ceremony will begin
at 17:30 for 18:00 on the 29th of February 2020, on the beautiful and rustic farm, Kleine Liefde.


Please note that there will be a cash bar available after the ceremony (presented proudly by our local brewer,
Indie Ale), so please remember to bring cash or cards. An alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverage for pre-drinks
and for the toast will be supplied, however, additional beverages will be supplied via the venue
at your own expense.


In the event that you would like to stay for a night or two, accommodation is to be arranged at your own
expense. In the accommodation section below, you will find details of several Hotels and B&Bs, also feel
free to have a look through the following accommodation sites, Travelstart, Lekkeslaap, Booking.com
or SafariNow for special rates.


As the theme of the wedding is 'Rustic', the dress code will range between smart-casual and formal
(no jeans or shorts please). Do, however, keep in mind that Porterville gets quite warm in the summer months.


We are so looking forward to having you there!


Taylor & Marla
