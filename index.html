Ceremony & Reception

Kleine Liefde, Porterville


We are very excited to invite you to join us in celebrating our union.
Have a look at the details below and let us know if you would be able to join us by clicking on the RSVP buttton.


The wedding will be held just outside Porterville on the beautiful and rustic Kleine Liefde Farm. The ceremoney will start at 5:00 pm 'till late.


Please note that there will be a cash bar available (proudly home-brewed by Indie Ale) after the ceremony, so remember to bring cash along. An alcoholic beverage to toast with will be supplied, any additional drinks are on own account.


Accommodation to be arranged on own account. In the accommodation section, you will find details of a few Hotels/Bnb's, in the event that you need to stay a night or two. Feel free to have a look at accommodation sites like Travelstart, Lekkeslaap, Booking.com or SafariNow for special rates.


As the theme for the wedding is 'rustic', the dress code will be smart-casual (no jeans please). Please also keep in mind that Porterville can get very hot.


We look forward to seeing you there!
